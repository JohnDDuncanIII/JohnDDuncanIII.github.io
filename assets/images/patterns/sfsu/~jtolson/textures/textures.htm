I created these background images for the world to enjoy. You may use them, but you may not take credit for them. The links below lead to 50 different swatch overviews. Each overview has 10 different swatches. Each swatch can then be demonstrated as a full background by selecting the swatch. I have tried to group the swatches by color and/or pattern.

Relax and take a dynamic tour of five of my favorite textures. The tour automatically presents the backgrounds, so don't forget to watch! You probably need to use Netscape 1.1 (or higher) for this to work.
